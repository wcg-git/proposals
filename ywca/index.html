03  /  What We Deliver

The work, in concrete terms.

Four phases take the honoree films from first call to ceremony screen.

01

Pre production and scheduling

We open with a kickoff call to lock creative direction, the interview question framework, and the look of the films. Then we coordinate with your team on honoree scheduling, grouping interviews into efficient filming days that respect seven busy calendars. Every location is scouted in advance so each setup is ready when the honoree arrives.

02

Interview filming days

A professional multi camera setup covering both interviewer and honoree, cinema lighting, and broadcast grade audio on both voices. We direct each conversation gently, keeping honorees comfortable so the moments stay real. Each day also captures supporting footage that gives every film room to breathe beyond the interview chairs.

03

Edit and story shaping

Every honoree gets her own arc. We cut each conversation to about two minutes, weave in supporting footage, color grade, mix audio, and add clean titles in the AWA look. Your team reviews online and we refine through revision rounds until each film is right.

04

Final delivery for every screen

Final masters arrive ready for the ceremony screen, plus web ready versions for your site, email, and social channels. Files come through a shared folder with clear naming, and we archive the masters for twelve months after delivery.
