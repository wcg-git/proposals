01  /  The Opportunity

One day captured. A whole build worth telling.

Thank you, Kristen, for having us out to the Eastpoint neighborhood kickoff. Capturing that day was a privilege, and the response to the recap said a lot about what this project means to the people around it.

Eastpoint is going to come out of the ground over the next few years, and there is a story worth telling at every stage. The building itself, and the impact it creates in the community around it. This is exactly the kind of work we do for developers like Holladay Ventures, and we would love to document Eastpoint the same way, so you have something powerful to put in front of partners, investors, and the public the whole way through.

03  /  What We Deliver

The work, in concrete terms.

Four ways the program shows up across the year.

01

Milestone capture visits

Planned shoot days at the moments that matter. Groundbreaking, structure rising, topping out, interiors, and the finished community. Ground and drone coverage on every visit, so the progress is felt from the street and from the air.

02

Milestone and recap films

Short, sharp films that mark each stage and a signature film that tells the full Eastpoint story to date. Built for partner updates, investor rooms, and public channels.

03

Story and impact pieces

Property and unit tours, plus the human side. The partners, the team, and the community Eastpoint serves. The pieces that turn a construction project into a story people care about.

04

Social cuts and a growing library

Vertical cuts formatted for your channels, plus a photography set from each visit. Every shoot adds to a library you own and can pull from for years.

07  /  Optional  /  Standalone Events

Groundbreakings and ribbon cuttings, covered in full.

The program documents the build. The big event days, a groundbreaking, a grand opening, a ribbon cutting, a community celebration, are their own production with their own crew and plan. We keep them separate on purpose. When one is on the calendar, send us the details and we will build that as its own proposal.

From live event direction and multiple cameras to drone and a same week recap, we produce event days end to end. The Eastpoint kickoff was the first one. Each future milestone celebration can get the same treatment, sized to the day.
